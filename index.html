How LeanOps fits into your factory

1. Supervisor console

A browser-based control panel where supervisors load jobs, assign workcenters, and see real-time status for each station.

2. Floor displays & tablets

Simple TV dashboards and operator tablets that show the next job, current queue, and status — no logins or complex menus.

3. Cloud data & KPIs

A lean data layer that tracks job completion, on-time performance, and output by station — ready for daily review and long-term improvement.

What you get out of the box

Supervisor dashboard

Manage jobs, stations, queues, and exceptions from one screen. Built to replace spreadsheets, sticky notes, and walk-arounds.

TV floor board

Clean, high-contrast display that shows live job status for the floor: what’s running, waiting, or blocked.

Operator entry UI

Lightweight interface for operators to update job progress and signal material or help requests without leaving their station.

Why manufacturers use LeanOps

Built from the floor up

LeanOps is designed by someone who actually runs production — not just consultants. Every screen is focused on helping real supervisors and operators do their jobs faster.

Start small, scale when ready

Begin with one department or workcenter. Once it’s working, expand to additional lines, processes, or sites without changing how people work.

No giant IT project

LeanOps is intentionally lightweight: browser-based, simple to host, and designed to integrate with your existing spreadsheets and ERP later if needed.

Measurable, daily impact

Track on-time vs late jobs, throughput, and station utilization so leadership can see real improvements week over week.
